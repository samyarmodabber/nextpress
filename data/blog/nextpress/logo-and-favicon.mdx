## Design your Logo

Creating a logo is an essential step in establishing your brand identity. Whether you're a business, a blog, or a personal project, a unique and professional logo will make you stand out. With [Canva](https://www.canva.com/), you can easily design a stunning logo. Canva offers a wide range of templates, fonts, icons, and customization options to help you create a logo that perfectly represents your brand.

## Resize PNG File

Once you have your logo, you might need to resize it for different purposes, such as social media, websites, or business cards. To resize your PNG files quickly and efficiently, use [Resize Image](https://onlinepngtools.com/resize-png). This online tool is straightforward and allows you to resize your images without losing quality.

## Convert PNG to SVG

Sometimes, you might need your logo in a different format, such as SVG, especially if you need a scalable vector graphic for various sizes and resolutions. To convert your PNG logo to SVG, you can use the [PNG to SVG Convertor](https://www.freeconvert.com/png-to-svg). This tool ensures that your logo maintains its quality no matter the size.
Choose a 100px by 100px png file and after convert it to svg, change the name of file to **logo.svg** and replace it with same name in folder `data/logo.svg`
