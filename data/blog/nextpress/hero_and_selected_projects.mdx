## 1. Enhanced Hero Section

The new Hero section offers a more striking and interactive way to captivate visitors right from the start. This feature includes:

- **Responsive Design**: The Hero section is fully responsive, ensuring it looks great on all devices.
- **Easy Customization**: With our intuitive interface, you can easily customize the Hero section to fit your needs without requiring extensive coding knowledge.

- **Active Options**: Enable users to Active or not Active on landing page.

This revamped Hero section not only enhances aesthetics but also boosts functionality, making it easier for visitors to navigate and interact with your content.

## 2. Project Showcase Module

Our new Project Showcase Module is tailored to highlight your portfolio with sophistication and clarity. Features include:

- **Project Details**: Each project includes a detailed description, images, and links to external resources.
- **Responsive Design**: The Project Showcase is fully responsive, ensuring a seamless experience across all devices.
- **Easy Customization**: Customize the appearance and layout of the Project Showcase to match your brand’s
- **Active Options**: Enable users to Active or not Active on landing page.

This module allows you to present your projects in a structured yet engaging manner, enhancing the overall user experience and making your portfolio more impactful.

## Active Options

In `siteMetadata.json` file add flollowing lines:

```json
landingPage: {
  ....
  showHero: true,
  showSelectedProjects: true,
},
```
